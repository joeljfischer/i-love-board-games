<h3>Review Policies</h3>
<p>This site is <i>primarily</i> a review site. If I don't feel that I've played a game enough‚Äîor in enough contexts‚Äîto provide a score and I still want to provide my thoughts, the review will be labeled an "impression" and un-scored.</p>

<p>I may be willing to test prototype copies and preview games (such as Kickstarter games), but I'm only going to preview games that I'm personally excited about. I only cover games that I actually have played a physical copy of, and I won't cover a game that I can only play digitally.</p>

<p>I do not accept money in exchange for previewing / reviewing a game, but I'm willing to receive a finished (or in some cases, prototype) copy of a game or games. I do not promise to play the game or provide a post for it. My reviews are always my own thoughts and I do my absolute best to be unbiased in my opinions. If I have any ties to a game or company, or if I receive a game for free, I will provide such a note in the post covering the game.</p>

<h3>Comment Policy</h3>
<p>Please be kind in comments. Don't make me turn them off, please. If you disagree with me, explain why, and never attack my, or anyone else's person or character for my, or their, opinions. That said, I love to hear why people love the games they do, even (and especially) when we disagree.</p>
